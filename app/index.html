About Us

Shovel Squad specializes in residential snow removal on privately owned properties. We service driveways, stairs, sidewalks and walkways. We’re a large network of local men and woman on call 24 hours a day, 7 days a week to assist with your winter needs. Anywhere you need snow cleared and de-icing, we can help!

Learn more from founder Isabelle Bailey’s interview with CTV.

Residential Snow Removal Services

Our innovative state of the art satellite app allows you to draw the area you need cleared so we can determine the square footage and give you an accurate cost. Since our weather is unpredictable, FULL refunds will be applied should you need to cancel.

Snow Clearing

Depending on the size of your property, we’ll dispatch a member of our shovel squad, our shovel equipped ATV, or our full size snow plow at the time you’ve requested us on your property. All service quotes include salting once the snow clearing is complete.

Salting Services

We use environmentally friendly, animal friendly and concrete friendly deicing agent made with organic ingredients only, to protect your home and our environment. You can also purchase a bag of our salting agent at the time of booking.

De-icing

We provide de-icing services for compact snow or ice buildup. Please inquire upon booking for additional costs associated with this service.

Do you want to apply to be one of our Shovel Squad helpers? Do you want to be part of a team that helps keep our city safe? We take care of our team members and always show our gratitude and appreciation with warm beverages, meals, incentives and bonuses. Please inquire within.

Requirements – MUST HAVE:

  • Own 4x4 truck / SUV
  • Snow tires
  • 24/7 availability through the winter season
  • Work-related references
  • Legal ability to work in Canada
  • Ability to make it to Port Coquitlam in 20 minutes in the snow

Responsibilities:

  • Quick response to dispatch at all hours
  • Clear snow on assigned properties (route supplied by dispatch)
  • Fill out log sheets and submit upon completion
